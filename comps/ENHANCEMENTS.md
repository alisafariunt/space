## 📝 Usage Instructions

### For Students
1. **Search:** Press `Ctrl+K` to find any theory instantly
2. **Read:** Scroll down, progress bar tracks your reading
3. **Jump:** Click any theory in TOC to navigate
4. **Theme:** Toggle dark mode for night studying
5. **Return:** Click ↑ button to go back to top

### For Exam Prep
1. **Quick Reference:** Use categorized TOC to find theories by domain
2. **Search Terms:** Type key concepts to locate relevant theories
3. **Print:** `Ctrl+P` for clean PDF export
4. **Highlight:** Links flash when jumped to - easy to find context
5. **Accessibility:** Full keyboard navigation for efficient study
