About Me

Bridging the gap between technology, business, and research

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Business Computer Information Systems at the G. Brint Ryan College of Business, University of North Texas. My journey into business and technology began with a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Management from the University of Isfahan, followed by a Master's degree in Industrial Engineering from Isfahan University of Technology.

As an entrepreneur, I founded several successful startups in the healthcare, sports, and e-commerce sectors. Leading Rasa Digital Marketing Agency, I have overseen a team of 20 professionals, delivering over 100 projects that helped businesses boost their online presence and refine digital strategies.

At UNT, my research focuses on how emerging technologies like generative AI can revolutionize digital marketing and e-commerce, aiming to enhance business efficiency while fostering sustainable practices in the digital economy.
